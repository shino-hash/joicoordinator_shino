Past Activities

Introduction to Japanese Calligraphy

October 26, 2024

Logan High School

Students learned the basics of Japanese calligraphy, focusing on brush techniques, proper posture, and the cultural significance of the art form. The workshop provided a hands-on opportunity to practice writing simple characters like "心" (kokoro - heart) and "夢" (yume - dream).

Origami Workshop

November 15, 2024

Utah State University

An engaging workshop where participants created various origami figures, including the classic crane and a samurai helmet. The session also covered the history of origami and its role in Japanese culture and festivals.

Japanese School Life Presentation

December 5, 2024

Logan Elementary School

A lively presentation for elementary students, giving them a glimpse into the daily life of a student in Japan, from school lunches to classroom activities and annual events.
